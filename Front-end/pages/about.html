About InterviewAI

Your Personal AI Interview Partner

Our Mission

InterviewAI was created to help people practice and improve their interview skills in a safe, realistic, and stress-free environment.

The goal of this project is to make interview preparation accessible to everyone — students, junior specialists, and career changers — regardless of their experience level.

Why I Built InterviewAI

I built InterviewAI after noticing that many capable people struggle during interviews not because of missing skills, but because they are not prepared for the interview format itself.

This project started as a personal initiative to create a tool where users can practice, make mistakes, receive feedback, and improve their answers without pressure or judgment.

AI-Powered Interview Simulation

InterviewAI uses AI-driven logic to simulate realistic interview questions and analyze user responses across multiple dimensions such as clarity, structure, confidence, and relevance.

Based on user input, the system provides structured feedback and personalized recommendations to help users improve and perform better in real interviews.

Personal Portfolio Project

InterviewAI is an independently developed project created as part of my personal learning journey and professional portfolio.

The project focuses on combining clean front-end design, thoughtful user experience, and future AI integration to solve a real-world problem.
